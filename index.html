About

Chitra is a London based YMCAfit level 3 certified yoga instructor teaching both mat and chair yoga. Her main motivation is to help people of all ages enjoy the invigorating feeling in their bodies that comes from performing yoga asanas.

In each class, Chitra endeavours to lead you on a journey of both body and mind - as you stretch and hold a posture, and your attention turns within, you start to feel the gratitude for your human body and the breath that powers it. Hopefully the enjoyment, increased flexibility and feeling of wellness will motivate you to keep practising.

Chitra has trained with gurus in India, and is experienced in working with those who have special needs, be it dementia, Parkinson’s or those on the Autistic spectrum. She's available for 1:1 classes and can work to suit any time zone you are in.

As well as being a yoga teacher, Chitra is a mum of three lovely daughters, a high school A-level maths teacher, a vegan chef and a physics graduate. Turmeric Yoga is Chitra's initiative to celebrate the power of yoga and plant-based cuisine for promoting health and well being.
